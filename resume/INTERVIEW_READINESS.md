# IT Project Manager Interview Readiness & Skill Improvement Schedule

A structured, daily and weekly execution roadmap designed to prepare for IT Project Manager, Technical Project Coordinator, and Product Operations roles.

---

## 📅 Weekly Focus Matrix

| Day | Focus Area | Daily Task List | Duration |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monday** | **Agile & PM Frameworks** | Review Agile/Scrum, Sprint Planning, estimation techniques, and Jira dashboard configuration. | 1.5 Hours |
| **Tuesday** | **AI & Automation Skills** | Practice using AI (Amazon Q, Claude) to draft PRDs, design checklists, and automate reporting tasks. | 1.5 Hours |
| **Wednesday** | **System Architecture & Flows** | Map out system workflows (e.g., Electric Dada 3-tier marketplace, eRestro QR order flow) using Mermaid or draw.io. | 1.5 Hours |
| **Thursday** | **Behavioral & Case Interviews** | Draft STAR-format stories for key achievements (e.g., coordinating 13+ platforms, resolving scope creep). | 1.5 Hours |
| **Friday** | **Mock Interviews & Q&A** | Practice answering common PM questions (e.g., handling resource conflicts, tracking SLAs) out loud. | 1.5 Hours |
| **Weekend** | **Weekly Retrospective** | Update profiles, analyze application conversions, and refine CV highlights based on market feedback. | 2.0 Hours |

---

## 🛠️ Daily Execution Checklist (Monday to Friday)

- [ ] **09:00 AM – 10:00 AM | Job Hunting & Applications**
  - Apply to 5-10 target roles on LinkedIn, Naukri, and Indeed.
  - Track application statuses in a central spreadsheet.
- [ ] **10:00 AM – 11:00 AM | Core PM Skill Drill**
  - Deep-dive into today's weekly focus area (refer to Focus Matrix above).
- [ ] **11:00 AM – 11:30 AM | AI Workflow Integration Practice**
  - Practice prompting and engineering workflow SOPs with LLMs to keep operations edge sharp.

---

## 📝 Key Interview Scenario Preparation (STAR Method)

Prepare 3-4 structured scenarios focusing on:
1. **The Multi-Platform Challenge:** Coordinating 13+ concurrent digital platforms at NexG.
2. **The Conflict Resolution:** Handling stakeholder/developer disagreements regarding timeline or scope on E-Restro.
3. **The System Optimization:** Designing a marketplace delivery workflow for Electric Dada that improved provider dispatch efficiency.
